Free coding classes
for public school students

What

  • 501(c)(3) non-profit
  • Free after school coding classes
  • We work with the system, students get SFUSD elective credit
  • Project based learning, more focused on practice than theory
  • Not a trade school

Why

  • Only 30% of SF public high schools have a CS class
  • Many professionals want to share knowledge
    … but not as a full-time educator
  • Businesses can and should work with the community

How

  • One full-time lead instructor per class
  • Utilize volunteers to provide more student support
  • Partner with other non-profits and the district
  • Recruit in schools to reach more diverse students
  • Provide laptops for use in class

Curriculum

Spring 2014

  • 3x Intro web dev (HTML, CSS, SVG, JS)
  • 1x Intermediate web dev (Ruby + Sinatra)

Metrics

  • 300% growth from Fall to Spring
  • >45 volunteer mentors signed up
  • >$25k raised from over 100 funders
  • Enough student signups to handle another class or two!

$$$

  • Administrative overhead (background checks, insurance, …)
  • Laptops & maintenance
  • Some lead instructor time is paid
  • Souveniers (hoodies, t-shirts, etc.)
  • Field trips and misc. hardware
  • Hoping to hire a full-time GM to scale up
  • Currently 100% funded by direct donation
